I write adult fantasy (mostly epic) with occasional forays into science fiction, stories that take world-building seriously and don't shy away from complexity. My work tends toward the atmospheric and the deeply grounded. I love exploring the gritty reality of how people actually live inside grand, sprawling systems, and how they push back when those rules no longer serve them. If you enjoy immersive settings, characters whose choices are fiercely driven by their world, and plot twists that truly reward your reading investment, you're in the right place.

I'm currently querying a novel and working on other projects at various stages. This site is a place for agents, readers, and fellow writers to find out more about my work and follow along with its progress.
