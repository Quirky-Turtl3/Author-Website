I write adult fantasy with occasional forays into science fiction — stories that take world-building seriously and don't shy away from complexity. My work tends toward the atmospheric and the morally intricate: characters who are neither heroes nor villains, in worlds that follow their own strange logic.

I'm currently querying my first novel and working on several projects at various stages. This site is a place for agents, readers, and fellow writers to find out more about my work and follow along with its progress.
