About my work

A brief introduction for agents considering my manuscripts.

Who I am

I'm Radu Paun, a writer of adult fantasy and science fiction. My work sits at the intersection of richly built worlds and intimate, character-driven stories — the kind of fiction that takes its internal logic seriously without sacrificing emotional depth.

I'm drawn to moral complexity, unreliable systems of power, and the quiet strangeness that lives at the edges of things. My fantasy tends toward the secondary-world and the atmospheric; my science fiction leans hard into the human cost of big ideas.

My writing

I'm currently querying my debut novel — a standalone adult fantasy. You can find a full overview of my active and upcoming projects, including writing and querying status, on the Projects page.
